Our Mission Statement


We are two physicians who became aware of the limitations of conventional medicine early in our careers, so we set out on a global journey to discover what true healing really is. We document and stream our encounters along the way, and in each episode explore a different type of medicine from around the world. In one of our episodes, we travel to South America to explore Amazonian shamanism, and in another episode, we head to Tibet to explore their traditional medicine. In this series, we ultimately discover sacred wisdom that deeply heals ourselves, humanity, and the planet. Follow along on this journey of the heart that will transform your life.With love and gratitude,
